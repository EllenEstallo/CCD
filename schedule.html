Schedule of Religious Education Classes


Religious Education classes are held weekly from mid-September to early May for children who are home-schooled or attending the public schools.



Classes for Grades 1-7 are offered in two sessions:
  • every Wednesday, from 4:30 - 6 pm, and
  • every Wednesday, from 7 - 8:30 pm.
Both sessions are held at
Jamesburg Presbyterian Church
175 Gatzmer Ave Jamesburg, NJ 08831
(See map on the right.)
